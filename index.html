Welcome

The Aging Lexicon Project seeks to understand adult age differences in the development of lexical and semantic knowledge.

This webpage is the result of the Symposium on The Aging Lexicon that took place in Basel, Switzerland, June 7-9, 2018, and gathered experts in Psychology, Linguistics, Neuroscience, and related fields to discuss open issues concerning the aging lexicon. Learn more about the proceedings of this symposium from our joint publication.

This webpage aims to serve as a home for the ideas and resources collected during the symposium. Over time we hope to grow the collection of resources in order to accelerate future progress in this area.

If you have any suggestions for additions please leave a comment on GitHub.

Thank you!

Dirk U. Wulff, Simon De Deyne, Rui Mata, &
the Participants of the Symposium on the Aging Lexicon
